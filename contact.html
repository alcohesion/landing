Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Thealcohesion VPU

How do I become a member of Thealcohesion VPU?

+

Membership is primarily through referral by an existing member, via our outreach mass registration programs, or through Thealcohesion agents. This helps ensure a community aligned with our goals. During registration, you'll provide credentials like your preferred username, full name, ID/Passport details, and contact information.

What is the EAC Wallet?

+

The EAC Wallet is our bespoke financial system designed for secure and efficient transactions within the VPU and beyond. It provides a safe way to manage your digital assets and participate in the VPU economy.

How are investments managed in the VPU?

+

Investments are managed through our secure platform with transparent tracking and reporting. Members can participate in collective investment opportunities that align with our mission of building Alkebulan.

What are the membership contribution levels?

+

We offer various membership contribution levels to accommodate different financial capabilities while ensuring everyone can participate in building our collective future. Contact us for detailed information about contribution structures.

Is the VPU platform secure?

+

Yes, the VPU is built using the latest stable technologies with robust security measures. We prioritize the protection of member data and assets through advanced encryption and secure transaction protocols.
